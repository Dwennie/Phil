Mission Statement

  1. Promote Inclusivity: We strive to build a culture that embraces diversity and celebrates the unique perspectives, backgrounds, and identities of all community members.
  2. Prevent Harassment and Discrimination: We are committed to preventing all forms of harassment, discrimination, and bullying.
  3. Support and Advocacy: We offer confidential support services to those who have experienced harassment or discrimination.
  4. Policy Development and Implementation: We develop and implement comprehensive policies and guidelines that set clear expectations for behavior and promote a safe and inclusive environment.
  5. Awareness and Education: We engage in ongoing education and awareness campaigns to promote understanding, empathy, and respect.
  6. Collaborative Partnerships: We collaborate with other departments, organizations, and community groups to create a united front in promoting a safe and inclusive environment.
